Protecting Animals And The Land

by Joshua chang

Lots of animals are closing to extinction, and people are polluting the earth, what should we do? We will try to stop people from killing animals and also protect the whole earth.

When you are trying to protect animals, try to find the animal that you are trying to protect. Next, you have to observe what habitat the animal lives in. Then you have to add some useful resources to the habitat that can help the animal. For example, if I want to protect pandas, I can plant some bamboos to help the pandas.

Now we have to talk about stopping land pollution. Try to reduce the waste you have, and remember to throw your own trash away, never throw it on to the floor. You know the three Rs, reduse reuse, and recycle? They are the three main key words that are about stopping land pollution. Reduce means to make lesser of the waste you want to get rid of. Reuse means to use the object that you don't need again and again, like reusing a card board box and using it to make art and crafts. Recycle means to make the object that you no longer need and making them into other useful objects, for example, using old news papers into brand new pieces of paper. That is why you always see recycling bins along the streets outside. There are three bins, one is for recycling, while the second one is for raw materials and the last bin is for other wastes. The recycling bin should contain papers, plastic and other objects that can be recycled. The raw material bin should contain wood, metals, and sand. Raw materials are objects that can be used to make buildings and houses. The last bin should contain food, banana peels, and other wet objects that can't be recycled. There are bins every where people should throw their trash in the correct bins. Next time when you see a piece of trash on the ground, just pick it up and throw it in the bins. Every one has a part to do that can make the whole world clean.

I Hope that this could make the world a better place to live in.
